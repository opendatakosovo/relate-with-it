Who

A collaboration between Open Data Kosovo (ODK) and Center for Research, Transparency and Accountability (CRTA). This is a MIT Licensed project, the source code available here. The used datasets are available here and were graciously opened by the Ministry of Data challenge.

Idea

What happens when you give someone a figure like "$16.93 billion (PPP, 2014 est.)"? Nothing happens, the average citizen cannot comprehend that sum. Instead of monetary value, let's convert expenditure costs into units of stuff that people can relate to.

Engagement

Get people into conversation about government budgets by giving citizens the ability to appreciate the opportunity costs of government expenses. Compare the relative opportunity costs across different regions for a given government expenditure.
